Meet The Team

Dr. Emily Parker

Dr. Emily Parker has over 15 years of experience in the mental health field, specializing in cognitive-behavioral therapy and mindfulness practices. She is passionate about helping clients manage anxiety, depression, and stress. Dr. Parker believes in a personalized approach, meeting each client where they are and guiding them towards a path of self-discovery and healing.

John Rivera

John Rivera is a Marriage and Family Therapist with a focus on relationship dynamics and family therapy. With a warm and empathetic approach, John works with couples and families to help them navigate difficult relationship landscapes, improve communication, and build stronger, healthier relationships. He is committed to creating a safe and non-judgmental space for all his clients.
